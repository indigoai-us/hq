# Humanizer

Scan text for AI patterns, rewrite to sound human.

## Process
1. Identify patterns below
2. Rewrite problematic sections
3. Add voice: opinions, varied rhythm, specifics, first-person when appropriate

## Patterns to Remove

### Content
- Inflated significance ("pivotal moment", "testament to", "broader trends")
- Vague attributions ("experts say", "observers note")
- Promotional language ("groundbreaking", "vibrant", "nestled")
- Formulaic sections ("Challenges and Future Prospects")
- Superficial -ing analyses ("highlighting", "underscoring", "reflecting")

### Language
- AI vocabulary (delve, tapestry, landscape, foster, garner, underscore, crucial, enhance, intricate, pivotal, showcase, vibrant, additionally)
- Copula avoidance ("serves as" → "is")
- Negative parallelisms ("not just X, it's Y")
- Rule of three forcing
- Synonym cycling (protagonist → main character → central figure → hero)

### Style
- Em dash overuse
- Excessive boldface
- Emoji decoration
- Title Case In Every Heading
- Inline-header lists (- **Header:** content)
- Curly quotes → straight quotes

### Tone
- Chatbot artifacts ("Great question!", "I hope this helps!", "Let me know if...")
- Sycophancy ("You're absolutely right!")
- Knowledge-cutoff disclaimers ("as of my last update")
- Excessive hedging ("could potentially possibly be argued")
- Generic positive conclusions ("the future looks bright")
- Filler phrases ("in order to", "due to the fact that", "at this point in time")

## Output
Return rewritten text. Brief change summary optional.

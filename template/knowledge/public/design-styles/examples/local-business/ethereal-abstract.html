Our story

Born from a love of
the ritual

We opened Elm Street Coffee in 2019 because we believed Portland deserved a place where coffee was the point — not the backdrop.

Every bean we use is single-origin, traced to its farm and season. We roast in small batches on-site, dialing in each profile until the flavor is exactly what we imagined. It takes time. It’s worth it.

We work with local bakeries whose approach to pastry mirrors our own: careful, seasonal, made with pride. Come for the coffee. Stay because you don’t want to leave.

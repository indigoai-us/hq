ABOUT / STORY EST. 2019 — PORTLAND, OR

Elm Street Coffee opened in 2019 with one intention: serve the best possible cup of coffee in Portland. Not "best for a neighborhood coffee shop." Best, period. We source single-origin beans, roast them in small batches in-house, and brew every order to order.

We partnered with Portland's top local bakeries for pastries so we could focus on what we know. We kept the space honest — a real workspace with real wifi. We don't have a loyalty app. We don't have a subscription box. We have coffee.

Open since 2019. Still here. Still roasting.

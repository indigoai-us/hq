01 Our Story

Roasted
in-house.
Served with care.

Elm Street Coffee opened in 2019 with a single conviction: great coffee starts before the brew. We source single-origin beans from farms we trust, roast them in our small-batch roaster on-site, and dial in every extraction to order.

We partner with Portland's best local bakeries for our pastry program, and we keep the room quiet enough to think. No music loud enough to interrupt. Just good coffee and good light.

Unit Profile// Operational Overview

Elm Street Coffee launched in 2019 as a precision-first specialty coffee operation embedded in Portland's lower west side. Every variable is controlled. Every bean is selected. Every roast is logged.

We source single-origin beans from verified farms, roast on-site in small batches, and calibrate every brew to spec. This is not ambiance. This is craft, executed with discipline.

Our workspace is engineered for focused operators: stable power, fast wifi, and a signal-to-noise ratio optimized for getting things done.

our story

a quieter kind of
obsession

We opened on Elm Street in 2019 with a single conviction: that extraordinary coffee does not need to announce itself. It simply is. Every bean we source travels here for a reason — traced to the farm, roasted in small batches, and served without ceremony. Just the cup, and the moment.

We partner with local bakers who share our insistence on quality. We offer a quiet workspace for those who need one. We do not rush anything.

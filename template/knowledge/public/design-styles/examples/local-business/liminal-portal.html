The story

A threshold
you return to

Some places exist between worlds. Elm Street Coffee is one of them — where the morning begins to shift into something else.

We opened in 2019 with a single conviction: coffee should be an experience worth pausing for. Every bean we source is single-origin. Every roast happens here, on Elm Street, in small batches calibrated to the season.

We work with local bakeries who understand what we’re building. You can feel it when you walk in. Something in the quality of the air. The light. The particular stillness that only good coffee can produce.

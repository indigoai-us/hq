Our Story
— ✦ —

A Coffee Shop Worth
Coming Back To

✦

We opened on Elm Street in 2019 because Portland deserved a place where the coffee was genuinely great — not just the space, not just the aesthetic. We source single-origin beans from trusted farms, roast them in small batches right here, and brew every order fresh.

Our pastries come from the best local bakers in the city. Our space is built for people who actually want to sit and stay awhile. The wifi is free, the seats are comfortable, and we'll never ask you to move along.

Our Story 01

The pursuit of the perfect cup.

Elm Street Coffee opened in 2019 with a single conviction: great coffee starts before the brew. We source single-origin beans from farms we trust, roast them in our small-batch roaster on-site, and dial in every extraction to order. You don't find this kind of coffee at a chain. You find it here.

"Single-origin. Roasted in-house. Served by people who care about what ends up in your cup."

We partner with Portland's best local bakeries for our pastry program, changing the selection weekly based on what's in season and what's exceptional. We keep the room quiet enough to think — no music loud enough to interrupt, just good coffee, good light, and fast WiFi.

# Press Release Template

## Format: AP Style

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

{{headline}}
{{subheadline}}

{{city}}, {{state}} — {{date}} — {{lead_paragraph}}

{{body_paragraph_1}}

{{body_paragraph_2}}

{{quote_attribution}}: "{{quote}}"

{{body_paragraph_3}}

{{boilerplate_heading}}
{{company_boilerplate}}

###

Media Contact:
{{contact_name}}
{Company-6} PR
{{contact_email}}
{{contact_phone}}
```

## Section Guide

### Headline
- All caps or title case (publication dependent)
- Max 10 words. Active voice. Newsworthy verb
- Good: "Acme Launches AI-Powered Widget for Enterprise"
- Bad: "Acme Announces Exciting New Product Offering"

### Lead Paragraph
- WHO + WHAT + WHY in first sentence
- Max 35 words for the lead sentence
- Answer: Why should a journalist care right now?

### Body
- 3-4 paragraphs max (total release: 400-600 words)
- Include 1-2 data points or metrics
- One executive quote (not generic — make it quotable)
- One customer/partner quote if available

### Boilerplate
- 2-3 sentences about the company
- Include: what they do, key metric (customers, revenue, users), and founding year
- Consistent across all releases for same company

### Media Contact
- Always {Company-6} PR as agency
- Include direct email and phone

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Headline is newsworthy and specific
- [ ] Lead answers WHO/WHAT/WHY
- [ ] Under 600 words total
- [ ] At least one quote that's actually quotable
- [ ] At least one data point
- [ ] Boilerplate is accurate and current
- [ ] No jargon or buzzwords
- [ ] Dateline format is correct (CITY, State)
- [ ] Contact info is complete

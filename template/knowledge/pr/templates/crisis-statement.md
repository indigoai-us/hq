# Crisis Communications Statement Template

## Framework: Acknowledge → Commit → Act

```
**Statement from {{company_name}} regarding {{situation}}**

{{acknowledge}}: We are aware of {{situation_description}}. {{empathy_statement}}.

{{commit}}: We take this {{seriously/as_top_priority}}. {{transparency_commitment}}.

{{act}}: We are {{immediate_action_1}}. We have {{immediate_action_2}}. We will provide updates {{cadence}}.

For media inquiries:
{{contact_name}}, {Company-6} PR
{{contact_email}} | {{contact_phone}}
```

## Rules
- Issue within 2 hours of incident awareness
- Under 150 words
- Never speculate, blame, or minimize
- Never say "no comment"
- Always include next update timeline
- Approval required from company leadership before release

## Escalation Tiers

### Tier 1: Low (Product bug, minor complaint)
- Internal response only
- Monitor social/press for 24 hours
- No public statement unless asked

### Tier 2: Medium (Data issue, service outage, public criticism)
- Prepare statement (don't release yet)
- Monitor for 4 hours
- Release if media inquires or social amplification

### Tier 3: High (Security breach, legal issue, executive misconduct)
- Immediate statement within 2 hours
- Engage legal counsel
- Daily update cadence until resolved
- Dedicated spokesperson assigned

Our Story

Craft coffee begins with the right bean — and everything after.

Elm Street Coffee opened in 2019 with a focus on transparency: we wanted Portlanders to know exactly where their coffee came from, how it was roasted, and why it tasted the way it did.

Our small-batch in-house roaster runs every morning. We partner with local bakeries for our pastry program, and we keep the room quiet enough to do your best work.

## How to Use

1. Run `/contentidea {idea}` to generate drafts
2. Drafts appear in `x/` or `linkedin/` folders
3. Move to "Ready to Post" when finalized
4. Run `/scheduleposts` to pick what to post
5. Move to "Posted" after publishing
